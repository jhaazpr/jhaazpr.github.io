About
I am a senior at UC Berkeley majoring in Computer Science, I am focused on research in human-computer interaction (HCI). I am particularly interested in design tools for architects and other creative applications. My research interests lie in computer graphics, human-centered engineering, and principles from architecture.

I currently do research under Eric Paulos in the Hybrid Ecologies Lab. For the past year, I have been a teaching assistant for CS160: User Interface Design and Development and am currently serving as head teaching assistant.
