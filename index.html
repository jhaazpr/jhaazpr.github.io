About
I am a recent graduate from UC Berkeley majoring in Computer Science. I do research in human-computer interaction (HCI), and am particularly interested in design tools for complicated creative design tasks. My research interests lie in computer graphics, human-centered engineering, and principles from architecture.

I am currently working as an intern at Adobe Research advised by Morgan Dixon and Mira Dontcheva. As an undergraduate at UC Berkeley, I worked with Cesar Torres and Eric Paulos in the Hybrid Ecologies Lab. I also served as both a head teaching assistant and a teaching assistant for CS160: User Interface Design and Development.
